About Midnight Safari Club

✦

A fictional luxury cocktail club inspired by safari elegance, exceptional spirits, and unforgettable nights in the heart of London.

The Lounge

An Escape Above the City

✦

Some evenings deserve a room that matches them.

The Midnight Safari Club lounge is built for moments that slow down time — intimate, unhurried, and entirely removed from the world outside.

This is where the evening begins. And where guests rarely want it to end.

The Bar

Craft Without Compromise

✦

Behind the bar, a leopard watches. Above it, over one hundred rare spirits wait to be discovered.

Our back bar is a collection built over time — rare Scotch whiskies, aged Cognacs, small-batch gins and premium vermouths. The cocktail list is short by design. Everything on it is worth ordering.

Every drink is built with the same precision a master jeweller brings to a single stone.

Visual Identity

Design Philosophy

✦
■

Deep Black

The colour of the bush at midnight. A backdrop that makes everything else glow.

◆

Amber Light

Warm tungsten and candlelight. The only lighting that makes a cocktail look alive.

✦

Gold Accents

Restraint. One thread of gold through every surface — never gilded, always present.

○

Crystal Glass

The vessel matters. Cut crystal catches light in a way no other material can replicate.

✾

Paw Print

The motif that runs through everything. A reminder of what inspired the whole venture.

Midnight Safari Club is a fictional luxury hospitality concept created as a design and branding portfolio project.
